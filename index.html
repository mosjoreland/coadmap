About Me

My name is Joseph Moreland, but the people that really know me call me by my middle name, Kawai. The point of this website is for you to get to know me and my work, so we could start off by you getting to know how I prefer to be addressed. I'm a 23 year old aspringing web developer based in Downtown Los Angeles. I've always been intrigued by the way user interfaces are designed and decided to take my interest further by learning how they operate. I have always had a love for art, and expressed my interest through editing photos and videos I would take for fun. I now wish to apply this interest technically, through web development and design. Here you will soon find some of my favorite works of art that I have created. Please enjoy and express any interest you have by contacting me through the links towards the bottom of the page!
